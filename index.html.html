Our Story

A True Hill Retreat
in Yelagiri Thayalur

Nestled in the peaceful Thayalur Village of Yelagiri Hills, Sunanthikaa Hill View Resort offers an authentic hill-stay experience — far from the noise of city life. Surrounded by nature, cool breezes and stunning valley views, every moment here is meant to refresh your soul.

Whether you're a couple seeking privacy, a family on holiday, or friends wanting to enjoy BBQ under the stars — this is your perfect escape.

Mr. Boopathy
Owner, Sunanthikaa Hill View Resort

Dining

Homemade South Indian Food

🍛
3 Meals / Day
Included

We serve freshly prepared authentic South Indian food three times a day — cooked with love in our kitchen. Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available.

🌅
Breakfast
Idli, dosa, upma, pongal — classic South Indian morning fare with filter coffee & tea.
☀️
Lunch
Full rice meals with sambar, rasam, curries (veg & non-veg), papad and more.
🌙
Dinner
Chapati, rice, gravies and sides — warm comforting meals to end a perfect day.
🍖
BBQ & Self Cooking
BBQ grill available for night sessions. Full kitchen access for guests who want to cook.

Getting Here

Find Us in the Yelagiri Hills

1
From Chennai (~220 km) Take NH44 towards Ambur. Exit at Vaniyambadi, follow signs to Yelagiri Hills. Resort is in Thayalur Village, Mangalam Post.
2
From Bengaluru (~160 km) Take NH44 towards Krishnagiri then Dharmapuri. Head to Yelagiri via Jolarpet. Follow directions to Thayalur Village.
3
By Train Nearest station: Jolarpettai (JTJ) – about 20 km away. Cab / auto available from station to resort.
4
Need Directions? Just Call! Call 94448 07448 — we'll guide you right to our doorstep.
